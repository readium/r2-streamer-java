[OPTIONAL] mo-esc-030 Section Escapability - Nested

Tests whether nested escapability is supported.

While Media Overlays (MO) are playing within the nested magenta-bordered escapable element below, invoke the 'escape' command (assuming it is provided by the reading system), then invoke it once again from within the top-level blue-bordered escapable element.

This is a top-level escapable document fragment, identified by the epub:type="glossary" attribute (other values should also be supported, this is for illustration purposes only).

This is another escapable document fragment, nested within the top-level one. Upon invocation of the 'escape' command in this nested element, MO playback should skip the remainder of this fragment, and continue at the next paragraph below (which is itself located within a top-level escapable fragment).

This is the remainder of the nested escapable document fragment, containing filler text to allow time to invoke the 'escape' command...

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua. Ut enim ad minim veniam, quis nostrud exercitation ullamco laboris nisi ut aliquip ex ea commodo consequat.

This paragraph immediately follows the nested escapable document fragment. Upon invocation of the 'escape' command here, the remainder of this top-level escapable fragment should be skipped, and playback should continue at the "normal" paragraph down below.

This is the remainder of the top-level escapable document fragment, containing filler text to allow time to invoke the 'escape' command...

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua. Ut enim ad minim veniam, quis nostrud exercitation ullamco laboris nisi ut aliquip ex ea commodo consequat.

This is a normal paragraph, immediately following the top-level escapable document fragment.

The test passes if MO playback escapes from the nested magenta-bordered escapable element, and resumes immediately after it (within yet-another escapable fragment), then escapes from the top-level blue-bordered escapable element, and resumes at the following "normal" paragraph.
